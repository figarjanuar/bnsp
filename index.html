Few Words About Us

Bikelah was founded in 2020 by a group of motorcycle enthusiasts who wanted to create a community where riders could share their passion for bikes. We started as a small group of 15 friends who loved going on rides together around the local area on the weekends.

Over the years, we have grown into an active community of over 500 members with riders of all ages and skill levels. Bikelah welcomes anyone who shares our interest in motorcycles, regardless of what bike they ride.

Our members come from diverse backgrounds, from college students to professionals across different industries. But we are all united by one thing - our love for riding!

As a club, we organize various events focused on riding for enjoyment, safety, and building camaraderie within our community. This includes regular day rides every weekend exploring scenic routes near our city, touring trips to farther destinations, and technical sessions to hone skills and share riding tips among members.

We also hold gatherings every month for members to get to know each other better, discuss motorcycling topics, plan future event agendas, and simply talk bikes over a meal!

With a commitment to riding responsibly and giving back, Bikelah partners with local charities and organizations on fundraising events involving bikes. We aim to promote motorcycling safety in our community as well. Our club motto says it best: Letâ€™s Ride Together!

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become the largest and best community of motorcycle riders in the Nusantara providing enjoyable and positive riding experiences for our members.

Mission

  1. Unite motorcycle enthusiasts and riders from various backgrounds.
  2. Organize regular monthly riding events and activities such as day rides, touring trips, and gatherings.
  3. Provide education on good, safe, and responsible riding to members and the general public.
  4. Actively participate and contribute to charitable and social events in the community.
  5. Build networks and good relationships with other motorcycle communities.
  6. Serve as a platform for members to share information and experiences related to the motorcycling hobby.

Products & Services

As a motorcycle riding community, we do not sell physical products. However, Bikelah provides the following services to our members:

Organized Motorcycle Rides

Our main offering is providing organized rides for members to explore routes safely in a group

Technical Sessions

We hold regular sessions usually on weekends to help riders improve their motorcycle handling skills and road safety knowledge.

Social Gatherings

Bikelah communities members through monthly gatherings involving activities

Membership Packages

We offer standard and premium annual membership packages with fees. Members enjoy discounts on rides, exclusive club merchandise, and other perks.
